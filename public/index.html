Our vision

Software is transforming the whole world. We will help you handle the infrastructure for it, let you focus on your application.

Our service

We are lazy people and passionate about automate everything.

DevOps CONSULTING

We helps you to architecute your infrastructure, on cloud or on premise. We know the inside out of AWS. Our developer all has AWS certificates. Monitoring, alerting, backup and disaster recovery, we got you cover. Want to deploy microservice? We will help you with Kubernetes, API gateways. Want to build your own PaaS, we can help you deploy Convox, Dokku.

Monitoring

Beside using monitoring service like Prometheus, Sensu, Icinga, we have our own monitoring tool noty.im which you can use free of charge when you are using our service. We help you integrate your application with our monitoring tools to get notify when service is down, when cron jobs weren't fired, when a worker job failed to finish..

Auto scaling

We help you architecture system to support auto scaling. Life is too short to scale manually. We try our best to design system that scale itself depends on metric. We also have our own framework to manage and binding for spot instance so that you can take advantage of low pricing.

Data migration

Migrate your data warehour, writing ETL job to transfer your data into a cloud solution like Redshift, DynamoDB...

24/7/365 on-call

Once you are our customer, we do 24/7/365 on-call rotation to help handle downtime or production issue. Shit happens. We needs to handle it fast.

Pen testing

We can help to conduct pen testing for your infrastructure with Metasploit.

Our feature products

We take advantage of our extensive experience helping start-ups or company build and monitor their infrastructure to build tool to make life easier for us, DevOps people. We focus on automation everything, building self-healing system.
